Bio

Grey Kupiec is a media designer based in New Haven, CT specializing in graphics and media for presentations, web, and other communications. Grey completed is MA in Media Studies at the New School in 2011, and since then has done work for Yale University, as well as for various local business and causes as a freelancer. Grey lives in the New Haven area with his wife and their cat, Ziggy.
